# **ADR-0004: Adopt L7 as the Semantic Layer Model**

**Status**

Accepted

## **Context**

AIR requires a consistent semantic classification for documents.

Without classification, documents become difficult to organize and interpret.

## **Decision**

Every AIR document shall belong to one primary L7 semantic layer.

Secondary layers are optional.

## **Consequences**

Benefits

- Consistent reasoning structure
- Easier AI interpretation
- Predictable document organization
